## Introduction

Teloscopy ("we", "our", or "the App") is a genomic health analysis application that provides biological age estimates, disease-risk assessments, personalised nutrition recommendations, facial-genomic analysis, and health checkup analysis. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

We act as the **Data Fiduciary** (as defined under Section 2(i) of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023) for all Personal Data processed through Teloscopy.

## Information We Collect

### Sensitive Personal Data or Information (SPDI)
- **Facial photographs** (biometric data): Used solely for facial analysis to estimate biological age, skin health metrics, ancestry inference, and pharmacogenomic indicators. Photos are transmitted to the Teloscopy backend server for processing and are **not stored** after analysis completes.
- **Health reports**: Blood tests, urine tests, and abdomen scans uploaded by you are parsed for relevant medical parameters. Reports are processed in server memory and **not persisted** after analysis.
- **Genetic variant data**: Self-reported SNP genotypes (e.g., rs429358:CT) used for disease risk assessment and pharmacogenomic analysis.
- **Telomere length measurements**: Derived from user-provided microscopy images for biological age estimation.
- **Health conditions and dietary restrictions** arising from medical conditions.

### Regular Personal Data
- **Demographic data**: Age, sex, geographic region, country, state, ancestry.
- **Dietary preferences**: Vegetarian, non-vegetarian, vegan, and other dietary patterns.

### Technical Data
- App preferences (theme, server configuration) stored locally on your device.

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or tracking data.
- Third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or social media trackers.
- Contacts, calendar, location, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

## How We Use Your Information

All data you provide is used exclusively to:
1. Perform the requested genomic, facial, and health analyses.
2. Generate disease-risk assessments and polygenic risk scores.
3. Create personalised nutrition and meal plan recommendations.
4. Analyse uploaded health reports and correlate findings with genetic data.
5. Display results within the App.

We do **not** use your data for advertising, profiling, or any secondary purpose.

## Consent

In accordance with Section 6 of the DPDP Act, 2023:
- We obtain **explicit, informed consent** before processing any Personal Data.
- **Separate consent** is obtained for facial image processing, health report upload, and genetic data processing.
- You may **withdraw consent at any time** (Section 6(6), DPDP Act) by contacting animaticalpha123@gmail.com.

## Data Processing and Storage

- **On-device**: App preferences are stored locally using Android DataStore. No health data is cached on device after you close the results screen.
- **Server-side**: All Sensitive Personal Data (facial images, health reports, genetic data) is processed **in server memory only** and is **not written to disk or any persistent storage**. Data is purged upon completion of each analysis session.
- **No cloud storage**: We do not store your data on any cloud service or database beyond the duration of a single analysis request.

## Data Security

In compliance with Section 8(5) of the DPDP Act and Rule 8 of the IT Rules, 2011:
- All network communication uses **HTTPS/TLS encryption** (TLS 1.2 or higher).
- Sensitive data is processed entirely **in server memory** and never written to disk.
- No user accounts or credentials are maintained.
- Security practices are aligned with **IS/ISO/IEC 27001** principles.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties — including insurance companies, employers, advertisers, or data brokers. Your data is never used for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing analysis results directly to you.

## Your Rights (DPDP Act, Sections 11–14)

You have the right to:
- **Access** (Section 11): Obtain a summary of your Personal Data and processing activities.
- **Correction & Erasure** (Section 12): Correct inaccurate data or request erasure.
- **Grievance Redressal** (Section 13): Lodge a grievance with our Grievance Officer.
- **Nomination** (Section 14): Nominate an individual to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity.
- **Complain to the Data Protection Board of India** if a grievance is not resolved.

## Children's Privacy

In accordance with Section 9 of the DPDP Act:
- Teloscopy is **not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age**.
- We do not knowingly collect data from Children (as defined under the DPDP Act).
- No tracking or behavioural monitoring of Children is undertaken.

## Health Disclaimer

**Teloscopy is NOT a medical device** and is not registered under the Drugs & Cosmetics Act, 1940. Results are statistical estimates for **research and educational purposes only** — they are not medical diagnoses. Nutritional recommendations are general guidance, not medical prescriptions. **Always consult a qualified healthcare professional** for medical decisions.
