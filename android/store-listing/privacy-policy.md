## Overview

Teloscopy ("we", "our", "the app") is a genomic intelligence platform that provides biological age estimation, disease risk prediction, personalised nutrition recommendations, facial-genomic analysis, and health checkup analysis. We act as the Data Fiduciary under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

This privacy policy explains how we handle your data in compliance with Indian data protection laws.

## Data We Collect

### Sensitive Personal Data or Information (SPDI)
- **Face photos** uploaded for analysis — transmitted to our server, processed in real-time, and **not stored** after analysis
- **Health reports** (blood tests, urine tests, abdomen scans) — parsed in memory, extracted values shown for your review, and **not stored** after analysis
- **Genetic variant data** (self-reported SNP genotypes, e.g., rs429358:CT) — processed for disease risk and pharmacogenomic analysis
- **Telomere length measurements** from microscopy images
- **Health conditions and medical dietary restrictions**
- We do **not** use your images or data for training machine learning models

### Regular Personal Data
- Age, biological sex, and ancestral region (used for analysis calibration)
- Country and state of residence
- Dietary preferences

### Technical Data
- Device type and Android version (for compatibility)
- App version
- Crash reports (if enabled)

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used **exclusively** to:
1. Perform genomic, facial, and health report analyses
2. Generate disease risk assessments (polygenic risk scores)
3. Create personalised nutrition recommendations and meal plans
4. Analyse and correlate health report findings with genetic data
5. Improve app stability (crash reports only)

We do **not**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising or marketing
- Share your information with insurance companies, employers, data brokers, or any other entity
- Store analysis results on our servers after delivery to your device
- Create behavioural profiles or track usage patterns

## Consent

In accordance with the DPDP Act, 2023 (Section 6):
- **Explicit, informed consent** is obtained before any data processing
- **Separate consent** is obtained for facial images, health reports, and genetic data
- You may **withdraw consent at any time** (Section 6(6)) by contacting privacy@teloscopy.app

## Data Storage

- Analysis results are stored **only on your device**
- The server processes all requests **in memory** and does **not persist** user data to disk or databases
- No user accounts are required to use the app
- Health reports are automatically purged from server memory after each analysis session

## Data Transmission

- All data is transmitted over **HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)** — encrypted in transit
- Data is sent only to the configured Teloscopy server
- No data is sent to third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services

## Your Rights (DPDP Act, Sections 11–14)

You have the right to:
- **Access** your Personal Data and processing information (Section 11)
- **Correct or erase** your data (Section 12)
- **Lodge a grievance** with our Grievance Officer (Section 13)
- **Nominate** an individual to exercise your rights (Section 14)
- **Complain to the Data Protection Board of India** if a grievance is unresolved
- Use the app without providing genetic data (profile-only mode)
- Delete all locally stored data by clearing the app's data or uninstalling

## Children's Privacy

In accordance with Section 9 of the DPDP Act, 2023:
- Teloscopy is **not intended for users under 18 years of age**
- We do not knowingly collect data from Children
- No tracking or behavioural monitoring of Children is undertaken

## Medical Disclaimer

**Teloscopy is NOT a medical device** and is not registered under the Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940 or the Medical Devices Rules, 2017. It is for **research and educational purposes only**:
- Disease risk assessments are **statistical estimates**, not clinical diagnoses
- Nutritional recommendations are **general guidance**, not medical prescriptions
- DNA sequence reconstructions are **research-grade inferences**, not verified genetic data
- **Always consult qualified healthcare professionals** for medical decisions
