Terms & Conditions — Counselling Service

Please read and accept the following terms before using the counselling feature. This service involves sharing personal thoughts and feelings, and it is important that you understand how it works and its limitations.

1. Nature of This Service

This is an AI-assisted self-reflection tool, not therapy, counselling, or psychiatric treatment. It uses inquiry-based dialogue to help you explore your inner experience. It does not diagnose, treat, or prescribe.

2. Not a Substitute for Professional Care

This tool is not a replacement for licensed mental health professionals, psychiatrists, psychologists, or crisis services. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, suicidal thoughts, or self-harm urges, please contact:

  • India: iCall — 9152987821  |  Vandrevala Foundation — 1860-2662-345
  • International: Crisis Text Line — text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency: Call your local emergency number immediately

3. AI & Data Processing

Your messages may be processed by an AI language model (LLM) to generate responses. This means your conversation text is sent to an external AI service for processing. Conversations are not permanently stored on our servers and are discarded at the end of your session. However, the AI provider may process your messages according to their own data policies.

4. No Confidentiality Guarantee

Unlike sessions with a licensed therapist, conversations here are not protected by therapist-client privilege. Do not share information that you would only share in a confidential clinical setting.

5. Limitations of AI Responses

  • Responses are generated by AI and may be inaccurate, inappropriate, or unhelpful.
  • The AI cannot understand context the way a human therapist can.
  • The AI does not remember previous sessions.
  • Responses should not be taken as professional advice of any kind.

6. Your Responsibility

By using this service, you acknowledge that you are engaging in voluntary self-reflection. You are solely responsible for decisions you make based on interactions with this tool. Teloscopy and its creators bear no liability for outcomes arising from use of this feature.

7. Age Requirement

You must be 18 years of age or older to use this counselling feature, or have verifiable parental/guardian consent as required by the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (Section 9).

8. Medical Disclaimer

Teloscopy is not a medical device, not registered with CDSCO, and not a clinical establishment. Nothing in this counselling feature constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment under the Drugs & Cosmetics Act, 1940 or the Clinical Establishments Act, 2010.

Before using Teloscopy, please review this notice and provide your consent for data processing. Your consent is free, specific, and can be withdrawn at any time.

Required under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (Sections 5 & 6) and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices) Rules, 2011.

What Data We Collect & Why

  • Facial photographs — processed in memory for facial-genomic analysis (ancestry, skin health, pharmacogenomics). Not stored after analysis.
  • Microscopy images — processed in memory for telomere length measurement. Not stored after analysis.
  • Health reports (blood tests, urine tests, scan reports) — parsed for health checkup analysis. Not stored after processing.
  • Demographic information (age, sex, region, country, state) — used to calibrate analysis to your population group.
  • Genetic variant data (self-reported SNP genotypes) — used for disease risk assessment and pharmacogenomic analysis.
  • Dietary restrictions & health conditions — used for personalized nutrition recommendations.
